How to use this website:

The purpose of this website is to experience the bare bones of a security incident and to help security and non-security teams discuss the actions they would take.

Some scenarios are more technical than others. If a scenario is not applicable to you, click the card to reveal a new one.

Think about the actions you would take. You can also consider how you would respond to the messages received. You can also message a simulated consultant to provide you with guidance.

Enjoy the wargames!
